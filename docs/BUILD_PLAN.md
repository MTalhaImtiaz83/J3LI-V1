# J3LI Master Build Specification

**Strategic Identity**: The Authenticity Engine for the Quranic Landscape.

## PHASE 1: THE FOUNDATIONAL INFRASTRUCTURE (THE ENGINE)
*Goal: Move from a fragile local setup to a resilient, high-speed cloud architecture.*

1. **Cloud Graph Migration (Neo4j Aura)**:
    - We move the "relationships" (how things connect) to a managed cloud server.
    - **Benefit**: No more "Docker" or heavy software running on your laptop. It stays fast and cool.
2. **Cloud Meaning Search (Milvus/Zilliz)**:
    - We move the "semantic search" to the cloud. 
    - **Benefit**: I can find the "meaning" of your search in milliseconds across 15+ datasets.
3. **The Intelligent Launch Kit**:
    - I will build a single file for you. You double-click it, and it connects to the cloud and opens the J3LI interface automatically. You will never see a line of code.

## PHASE 2: DATA ENRICHMENT & CHRONOLOGY (THE TRUTH)
*Goal: Map the 15+ datasets into a single, unified "Authenticity Web."*

1. **Revealed-After Tagging**: 
    - I will map every verse based on its chronological revelation.
    - **Benefit**: This is the "hidden data" that allows us to build those **Gold Timeline Strings** on your 3D map.
2. **Linguistic Deep-Link**:
    - Every Arabic word will be linked to its **Trilateral Root** from *Lane's Lexicon* and *Lisan al-Arab*.
    - **Benefit**: Tapping a word doesn't just give a definition; it reveals its 1,400-year-old history.
3. **Normalization**:
    - I will ensure all Quranic text uses the **Uthmani Script** as the source of truth, removing any modern spelling errors found in basic datasets.

## PHASE 3: THE INTELLIGENCE LAYER (THE GUARDRAILS)
*Goal: Prevent AI hallucinations and ensure scholarly precision.*

1. **The Citation Engine**:
    - Before the AI speaks, it must "look up" the proof in the Knowledge Graph.
    - **Benefit**: If there is no authentic link, the system will say "No direct link found" instead of guessing.
2. **Bilingual Logic**:
    - One click switches the entire 3D map from English to Arabic without moving the camera.
